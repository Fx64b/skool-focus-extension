# Logo Generation Prompt for Skool Focus Extension

## Concept
A minimalist, modern logo for a browser extension that helps users focus while using the Skool.com learning platform by hiding distractions.

## Visual Direction
Create a simple, flat-design icon that combines elements of focus and learning/education.

## Prompt

```
Design a minimalist, flat-style logo icon for a browser extension called "Skool Focus".

Visual Elements:
- A target/bullseye or crosshair symbol representing focus
- Subtle incorporation of educational elements (book outline, graduation cap silhouette, or pencil)
- Clean, geometric shapes
- Centered composition that works well at small sizes (16px to 128px)

Color Palette:
- Primary: Warm golden yellow #F8D481 (rgb(248, 212, 129))
- Secondary: Slightly lighter yellow/cream #FFE196 (rgb(255, 225, 150))
- Accent: Deep warm brown or dark gray for contrast #2C2416

Style:
- Flat design, no gradients
- Smooth, rounded edges
- High contrast for visibility
- Professional and modern aesthetic
- Works well on both light and dark backgrounds

Technical Requirements:
- Square aspect ratio (1:1)
- Scalable vector-style design
- Clean edges and shapes
- Minimal detail (must be recognizable at 16x16 pixels)
- No text or letters in the icon

Mood:
- Professional
- Calm and focused
- Educational
- Purposeful and intentional
```

## Alternative Concept Variations

**Variation 1: Eye with Book**
- A stylized eye symbol (representing focus/attention) with a small book or page element integrated

**Variation 2: Zen Circle with Academic Element**
- An incomplete circle (Enso circle) symbolizing focus and zen, with a minimalist graduation cap or book in the center

**Variation 3: Lighthouse Beam**
- A simplified lighthouse with a focused beam of light, representing guidance and concentration in learning

## Usage Notes
- The logo should work at sizes: 16x16, 32x32, 48x48, and 128x128 pixels
- Will be used as browser extension icon in Chrome, Firefox, and Safari
- Should maintain clarity when displayed on browser toolbars
- Must be distinctive enough to stand out among other extension icons
